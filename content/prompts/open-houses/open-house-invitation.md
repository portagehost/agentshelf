Write an engaging open house invitation [for social media / email / text / flyer].

Property details:
- Address: [street address]
- Price: [listing price]
- Key features: [3-5 standout features like "chef's kitchen," "backyard oasis," "smart home"]
- Open house date/time: [day, date, and time]
- Special incentive: [if any - refreshments, door prize, etc.]

Highlight what makes this home special:
- Unique selling point: [what sets it apart]
- Who it's perfect for: [growing families, downsizers, first-time buyers, etc.]

Tone: Exciting and inviting without being hypey. Create urgency (limited showing opportunity) and FOMO. Make them want to come see it in person.

Include a clear call-to-action and your contact info.

Keep it concise - under 100 words for social media, 150 words for email.
