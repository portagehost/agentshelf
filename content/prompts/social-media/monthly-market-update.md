Write a social media post sharing this month's local real estate market update.

Market data to include:
- Location/area: [your market]
- Average sale price: [amount or % change]
- Days on market: [average number]
- Inventory levels: [low/balanced/high]
- Notable trend: [e.g., "first-time buyers returning," "luxury market heating up"]

Tone: Informative and authoritative but easy to understand. No jargon. Help followers understand what this means for them as buyers or sellers. Include a call-to-action to reach out for a personalized market analysis.

Keep it under 120 words and suggest 3 relevant hashtags.
