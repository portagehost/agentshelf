Write a detailed neighborhood overview for buyers considering [neighborhood name].

Information to include:
- Overall vibe/character: [describe the feel - family-friendly, urban, quiet, trendy]
- Types of homes: [typical styles and price ranges]
- Schools: [quality, names if you know them]
- Amenities within 10 minutes: [grocery stores, restaurants, parks, gyms, coffee shops]
- Commute considerations: [distance to downtown/major employers, traffic patterns]
- What residents love most: [unique features or benefits]
- Potential concerns: [be honest - noise, parking, etc.]

Audience: [specify - first-time buyers, families, retirees, investors]

Tone: Informative and balanced. Paint a vivid picture of daily life there. Be honest about pros and cons - this builds trust. Help them imagine themselves living in this neighborhood.

Keep it 300-400 words.
