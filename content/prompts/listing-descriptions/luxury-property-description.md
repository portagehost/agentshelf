Write an elegant, sophisticated property description for a luxury listing.

- Price: [listing price]
- Bedrooms/Bathrooms: [numbers]
- Square footage: [size]
- Luxury features: [list 5-7 high-end features like "chef's kitchen with Sub-Zero appliances," "infinity pool," "smart home automation," "wine cellar," "private gym"]
- Architectural style: [modern, traditional, Mediterranean, etc.]
- Views or setting: [oceanfront, mountain views, private estate, etc.]

Target buyer: [e.g., "executive," "second-home buyer," "empty nester"]

Tone: Sophisticated and aspirational without being pretentious. Use elegant language that evokes emotion and lifestyle. Focus on the experience of living there, not just features. Emphasize privacy, quality, craftsmanship, and exclusivity.
